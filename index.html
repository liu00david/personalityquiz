HCER Relationship Typology

Discover your 4-letter romantic personality type

Most personality tests focus on work or general traits, but how you love is just as important as who you are. The HCER quiz measures four core dimensions of romantic behavior to help you understand your relationship style.

The Four Dimensions

  • [H] Hope - Idealist vs Realist
  • [C] Connection - Physical vs Emotional
  • [E] Expression - Social vs Private
  • [R] Resolution - Forgiving vs Critical
